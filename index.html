Frequently Asked Questions

What is the pricing?
Our pricing is very affordable and designed for small businesses. It is just ₹39 monthly, or you can opt for the yearly plan at ₹399.
How long does it take to onboard my staff?
Setup takes less than 10 minutes. You generate unique staff IDs from your dashboard, hand them to your employees, and they can start logging tasks immediately.
Do my employees need to download an app?
No. Zenda works perfectly in any mobile web browser. Your staff just visits the website, enters their ID, and they are in.
Can I manage multiple godowns/locations?
Yes. You can add as many locations as you need and track stock movement between different godowns effortlessly.
