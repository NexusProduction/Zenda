✦ Features

Everything your team needs

A complete business OS — from task management to real-time notifications and built-in productivity apps.

👥

Multi-Role Access

Owner, Manager, and Staff roles with different access levels. Each member gets a unique ID card with their designation.

📋

Smart Task Assignment

Assign tasks to any team member with one click. Get real-time updates when tasks are completed or declined.

🔔

Live Notifications

Owners see every login. Staff get notified when tasks are assigned. Everyone stays in sync, always.

🪪

Digital ID Cards

Auto-generated beautiful ID cards for every team member. Download as PNG and share directly via WhatsApp.

📱

Built-in Mini Apps

Calculator and Calendar apps built right in. All data syncs across devices — log in anywhere, pick up where you left off.

🔐

Secure OTP Login

Email OTP verification for every login. No passwords to remember for staff — just your unique ID and email.
