Frequently Asked Questions

What is the pricing?
Our pricing is highly affordable and designed specifically for small Indian businesses to scale. It is just ₹39 monthly, or you can opt for the yearly plan at ₹399. No hidden per-user fees!
How long does it take to onboard my staff?
Setup takes less than 10 minutes. You generate unique staff IDs from your master dashboard, hand them to your employees, and they can start logging tasks immediately.
Do my employees need to download an app?
No. Zenda works perfectly as a Progressive Web App (PWA) in any mobile web browser. Your staff simply visits the website URL, enters their secure ID, and they are into their portal.
Can I manage multiple godowns/locations?
Yes. You can add as many locations as you need and track stock movement between different godowns effortlessly using our transfer module.
