Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to onboard my team?
Most companies are fully set up within 24 hours. You simply upload your CSV inventory list, generate staff codes, and you're ready to operate.
Do my staff need to download an app?
No, Zenda is a progressive web app. Your staff can simply log in via any web browser on their mobile devices without needing to download or update anything.
Can I manage multiple warehouse locations?
Yes. Zenda natively supports multiple "Godowns" or locations. You can track inventory transfer between locations seamlessly.
Is my data secure?
We use enterprise-grade encryption for all database records. Furthermore, your staff only have access to the specific modules you grant them permission to see.
