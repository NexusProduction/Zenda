Frequently Asked Questions

What is the pricing?
Our pricing is highly affordable. Just ₹39/month or ₹399/year. No hidden per-user fees — your entire company, one flat price.
How long does it take to onboard my staff?
Setup takes less than 10 minutes. Generate unique staff IDs from your master dashboard and hand them to employees. They start immediately.
Do my employees need to download an app?
No. Zenda works as a Progressive Web App (PWA) in any mobile browser. Your staff visits the URL, enters their ID, and they're in their portal.
Can I manage multiple godowns/locations?
Yes. Add as many locations as you need and track stock movement between different godowns effortlessly using our transfer module.
