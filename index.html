Built for how modern businesses operate

Every feature your team needs — task management, inventory, expenses, team calendar — in one place.

📋
Task Management
Assign tasks to specific team members with deadlines, priorities, and real-time status updates. Accept or decline with one tap.
👥
Staff Management
Add staff and managers, generate secure login credentials with ID cards, and control access levels across your company.
📦
Inventory Tracking
Manage multiple warehouses, track stock movements, get low-stock alerts, and view product-level entry logs.
💳
Expense Management
Record company expenses by category, set monthly budgets, approve or reject submissions, and track spending patterns.
📅
Team Calendar
Schedule events, set team reminders, and keep everyone aligned with a shared real-time calendar.
🔔
Smart Notifications
Get instant alerts for new tasks, approvals, and team activity. Accept or reject work directly from notifications.
