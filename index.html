Capabilities

Built for how
businesses operate

Every feature is deliberate. No bloat, no configuration maze.

Role-based access control

Three clean tiers with precise, non-overlapping permissions. Owner controls everything. Manager assigns and tracks. Staff receives and executes.

Owner · Full control Manager · Assign + track Staff · Receive + complete

Smart task assignment

Assign with due dates. Tasks auto-sync to the assignee's calendar. Accept, decline, or complete — every action is logged instantly.

Live notifications

Every login, task completion, and decline lands in real time. The owner sees everything. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Digital ID cards

Auto-generated, downloadable ID cards for every team member. Company cards, staff cards — branded, beautiful, shareable.

OTP-secured login

Three login paths, one standard: email OTP verified on every sign-in. Enterprise-grade security regardless of team size.

Built-in mini apps

Calculator with cloud history, Calendar synced across devices, Inventory management. Apps that work together, not in silos.
